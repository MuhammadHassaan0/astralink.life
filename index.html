Astralink

Conversations that bring memory to life.

Loneliness is becoming one of the quietest mental health crises of our time. We’re building Astralink to meet it head-on, not with feeds or content, but with presence.

Astralink lets you reconnect with the people you never wanted to lose. Using memory data — texts, recordings, and stories — we’re creating emotionally real conversations with AI versions of those who shaped you.

It starts with voice. You speak, and they speak back — in their tone, their words, their way. Not as avatars. Not as assistants. But as memory made present.

Over time, these AI personas will move beyond screens. Embodied. A presence. A familiar rhythm in the room. Not a gimmick — a memory you can sit beside.

This isn’t science fiction. It’s where memory, meaning, and machine learning converge.

Astralink is quiet by design. No ads. No tracking. No engineered addiction loops. Just a private, human space — built for grief, memory, and connection.

We’re based in Faisalabad, Pakistan. Moving to San Francisco soon. This is not just a company. It’s our life’s work.

If this resonates, reach out. We’re not hiring yet — but we will be.
